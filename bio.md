Lecturer at the [University of Science](https://www.fit.hcmus.edu.vn/), VNU-HCM ([VNU350 Project](https://vnu350.vnuhcm.edu.vn/)), expertise in Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence. Former Research Fellow at [NTU Singapore’s Air Traffic Management Research Institute](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/atmri) and Research Scientist at [Preferred.AI](https://preferred.ai/). PhD graduate from [SMU School of Computing and Information Systems](https://computing.smu.edu.sg/) under [Prof. Hady W. Lauw](http://www.hadylauw.com), with a dissertation on recommendation explanations that provide rationales for recommendations. Recipient of multiple prestigious scholarships and fellowships, including a [Distinguished Paper Award at IJCAI 2020](/assets/awards/Distinguished_Paper-Le.pdf). Passionate about advancing trustworthy AI and translating research into real-world impact.
